About

I am currently a Geographer at the Metric Geometry and Gerrymandering Group. I graduated from Macalester College in 2018 with a major in Geography and a minor in Data Science.
My professional interests are in GIS and voting rights. I am responsible for the collection and maintainence of MGGG's election geodata, available for download from the GitHub organization MGGG-States.
