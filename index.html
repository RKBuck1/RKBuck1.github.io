Ruth Krebs Buck

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Geography at Penn State researching the effects of political and adiministrative redistricting on power and outcomes for marginalized communities. I earned my MS in Geography from Penn State in 2022. Prior to grad school at Penn State, I received my BA in Geography from Macalester College with a minor in Data Science. I then spent two years as a researcher at the MGGG Redistricting Lab studying computational approaches to US redistricting.
